Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the AI analysis? +

Our AI uses advanced machine learning models trained on vast amounts of market data. While highly accurate for pattern recognition and technical analysis, remember that all trading involves risk and the AI should be used as a tool to support your decision-making, not replace it.

Can I use it on mobile devices? +

Currently, DynoTrade AI is available as a Chrome extension for desktop use. We're working on mobile apps for iOS and Android that will be released soon. The extension works perfectly on desktop Chrome browsers.

What happens if I run out of searches? +

You can purchase top-up plans to add more searches to your account. The 100-search top-up costs ₹349 and the 200-search top-up costs ₹649. Top-ups are only available for active subscribers.

Is my trading data secure? +

Yes, we use enterprise-grade encryption and security measures. We don't store your personal trading data or account information. All analysis is processed securely and data is not shared with third parties.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime? +

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. You'll continue to have access to the service until the end of your billing period. Any unused searches from your subscription will remain available until expiry.

Does it work with all chart types? +

Yes, our AI can analyze candlestick charts, line charts, bar charts, and various technical indicators across all supported platforms. It works with intraday, daily, weekly, and monthly timeframes.
